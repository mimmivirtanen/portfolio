About the project

Did you know that in Sweden 200 tons of textiles get burned every day?

Brief and idea

Beskriv briefen. Sedan: I chose to focus on how the clothing industry affects the climate. This led me to think about second-hand, reuse, and fast fashion. In my research, two points caught my attention: the first is that people order clothes online, often in multiple sizes of the same item, and then return them. It is cheaper for companies to discard returned items than to sell them again. Secondly, I realized that many find it complicated to shop for second-hand clothes online.
