Twist

Your digital fitting room

Year

2023

Role

UX/UI designer

Tools

Figma, Illustrator, Luma AI

About the project

Twist - your digital fitting room. An app where you can try on clothes directly on your 3D avatar, using the AI tool Luma.

In two simple steps you create an account, scan your body and then you are ready to try on clothes on your 3D-avatar.

Brief and idea

Did you know that in Sweden 200 tons of textiles get burned every day?

In this project I focused on how the clothing industry affects the climate. This led me to think about second-hand and fast fashion. In my research, two points caught my attention: the first is that people often order multiple sizes of the same item, and then return them. It is then cheaper for companies to discard returned items than to sell them again. Secondly, I realized that many find it complicated to shop for second-hand clothes online.

Twist will reduce this problem.

Process

I worked in Figma, Illustrator and Luma AI. Early on in the process I created a design system. And then there is text about the process, working with greyboxes, different programs. Etc.

Working with AI

To create the 3D-avatar I used Luma AI, an app where you can create lifelike 3D. I used my boyfriend Isaac as a guinea pig and scanned his body with my phone, using Luma. And then there is text about the process.
